I work at the intersection of cloud infrastructure, GPU workloads, and cost optimization. By day I'm a Senior DevOps engineer; by curiosity I'm a cloud physicist — experimenting with how to run AI/ML workloads faster, cheaper, and more reliably across providers.

## What I Write About

- **GPU workloads** — practical guides for running GPUs on AWS, GCP, Brev, Verda, E2E Networks, and your own machine. See the [Running GPUs in 2026](/docs/running-gpus-in-2026) series.
- **Cost optimization** — reducing cloud spend without hurting reliability or developer velocity.
- **Cloud infrastructure** — Kubernetes, containers, networking, and everything in between.
